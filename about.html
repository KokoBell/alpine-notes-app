Listy

About the app

Listy is an easy app you can use to keep track of your grocery basket and the total cost while shopping.

How to use it

Step 1:
Enter the name of the item.

Step 2:
Enter the price of the item.

Step 3:
Watch the magic! The app automatically updates the total and the number of items.

Step 4:
When you've bought an item, click it to check it off the list.

Step 5:
Double click an item to edit it.

More information

Listy is a PWA, meaning it is installable on any of your devices.

Once installed, you can use the app offline - not data, wifi or connectivity needed.
